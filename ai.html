AI Engineering

When people think of AI, they typically thingk of robots such as Glados from portal or Mettaton from Undertale. However, not all AI's are robots.

Introduction

What is AI? Good question. AI is short for artofficial inteligence. Basically, that means a program designed to simulate the human thought process. AI is everywhere. From the software that recommends you videos online to Tesla's autopilot. Those who design these services are known as AI engineers.

Payscale

Entry level positions start at arjund 80K. However, some high-paying positions can go up to a wapping 300K.

Programming languages

The most common programming languages in AI are C++ and Python.
AI engineers also need to know how to use programs to organize their data. FOr example, MongoDB, Hadoop, and Apache Spark. Frameworks such as PyTorch will also need to be learned, and you need a good understanding of math and machine algorithms as well.
