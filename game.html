Game Development

One of the most talented game devs in the word is the developper of Roller Coaster Tycoon, who coded their whole game in assembly.

Introduction

Minecraft, Undertale, Animal Crossing, and Sonic the Hedgehog. What do all of these games have in common? They were all made by talented individuals known as game developers. They are responsible for creating and maintaining the games that we all know and love. Depending on the size of the team, game developers may also play a role in game storylines, art, and even music.

Payscale

Entry-level positions start at about $65,625. As one progresses in their career, they can go up to 108,625.

Pros and cons


Pro Con
It can be really rewarding designing a game. If you are in a large team, you may not have an opportunity to get creative.
It is a low-risk career due to the demand for good developers. Sometimes tasks can get extremely repetitive.
As a game developer, there is always something to learn. New languages, mechanics, and opportunities to implement new logic and easter eggs. You need a lot of patience in order to persue this career. Debugging and testing games can be time-consuming and dificult.

Programming languages

The programming language you use can heavily depend on where the game is being played. On modern consoles and PC, C++ is the go-to. You can also use languages like Java or Python on PC. When developping for iOS, you will probably be using swift. Android, Cotlin or Java.
