Mobile app development

Mobile app developers create the apps we use every day.

Introduction

Mobile app developers are responsible for the creation of apps on mobile opperating systems such as iOS or Android.

Payscale

The entry level salary in this position is $49,000.
The highest paying salary is about $97,000.
Salaries in freelance positions of this nature could average a lot higher depending on amount of jobs taken, mostly due to the nature of freelancing and money not going to a whole group of people.

Pros and cons


Pro Con
Wide range of opportunities. From building your own app to working for a company, and also freelancing. Developping your own apps is expensive. Buying graphics, sound libraries if neccessay, as well as a lisence on iOS.
Mobile apps are widely used, therefore the demand is high. High demand also means high competition. This can make it hard for new developpers.
Apps allow your users to take advantage of the features of your device. Users may be hesitant to download your apps, making the business quite chalenging if you choose to go an alternative rout without an employer.

Types of mobile ap developers

There are a few dfferent types of mobile app developers. iOS and Android are the main two, but there are also developers who work with both OS's.

Programming languages

iOS

When developing on iOS, you must know either Swift or Objective C. Swift is newer and actively maintained by Apple, therefore it is prefered over Objective C.

Android

Android's play store excepts a wide range of programming languages. The most common languages used are Java and Kotlin, but Basic and C++ are also used in rare occasions. The prefered language used by Android developers in 2022 is Kotlin.

Hybrid

IF you are creating apps on iOS or Android, the main language you will wour with is React Native.
