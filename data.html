Data Science

Data scientists work with a ton of graphs and charts in their career.

Introduction

Everything we do with technology involves data. Data scientists are responsible for analysing said data and using it to give insite to or improve businesses.

Payscale

The average entry-level salary for a data scientist is $77,450. For axperienced data scientists, that can go up to $134,550.

Pros and cons


Pro Con
Due to its versatility, you have the opportunity to work in a variety of fields The lines between data to analyse and the privacy of customers is blurry. You have to be careful not to craoss it
You have the opportunity to save lives, especially when working in the healthcare secter. There are many branches of data science, which means that it is impossible to truely master it.
Good data scientists are highly in demand. You may come across companies whose data shows extreme bias or holes due to poor management. This can yield unexpected or unhelpful results.

Programming languages

One of the best programming languages for data science is Python. Due to its vast number of libraries, Python is capable of both visualizing and analysing data. Some of the most popular python libraries are NumPy, Matplotlib, and TensorFlow.
Another widely used language is SQL. It is used in databases, and despite there being a variety of data systems to work with such as SQLite and MySQL, their syntax is extremely similar, which makes it really easy to learn.
There are a lot more languages that can be used in data science but these two are some of the more popular ones.
